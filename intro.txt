                Welcome in TriCity Trolley Tour!

In this extremely simple text-based game, You, my dear Player, will be driving
an imaginary trolley traveling from station to station along the SKM railway
line.

Your journey begins on the Gdańsk Śródmieście station and the farthest train
station you can arrive is Gdynia Główna.

I highly believe that everything else will be self-explanatory.

I wish you good luck and have nice trip!
